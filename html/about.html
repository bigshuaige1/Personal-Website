CREATION CONCEPT

I dismantled the design into four parts, "Touch", "Introduction", "Connection", and "Same".     "Touch" refers to the human body's tactile experience of feelings, time, space, environment, impressions, etc.;     "Introduction" refers to the designer's integration and collocation of tactile experience, including but not limited to extending human feelings about the outside world (nature, heaven and earth) to creation, and introducing human emotional memory into space.     "Connection" refers to the designer's role of "mediation" to make the creation become the echo and continuation of the environment and history, so that the human body's feelings are mapped to the environment and reconnected with the creation.     Under the construction and interweaving of "connection", "sameness" is achieved, that is, it is integrated with people, heaven and earth, creation, past, future, etc.
